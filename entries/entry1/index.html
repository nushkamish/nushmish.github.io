Look Out.
I walked into my new room anticipated to find out what my view for the next 365 days is going to be like. I look out and between the buildings, I see the river. The east river pier is a two- minute walk from my house, I see it from my window dusk and dawn. Same blue sky and water, different thoughts every time.

It's quiet there. Well, not really, you can still hear the sirens and the screams of the city but the sounds of the water takes over. You can smell the water, hear the soft waves, I can hear my thoughts, what have I done this past week?
