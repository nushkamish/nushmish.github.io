10/09/2023

A Suburban Sunday with Sai.
I've heard so many people say "I need to get out of the city." Well, this week it was my turn. I NEEDED to get out of the city. Not the city, maybe, just my thoughts. I have a lovely roommate who offered me a train ride and a weekend in Princeton and like you would assume by now, I jumped at that offer. It's funny, I usually go down to the East River to "escape my thoughts", the river was *NOT* enough this week. I felt homesick, homesick for who I was. Who was I? Who were we all? Kids. I saw a lot of children this weekend. I have a weird feeling when I look at kids these days. I wanna be them again!
